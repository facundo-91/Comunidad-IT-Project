Anastasia is a violin that was given to Lindsey by her Russian fans. There are no videos of her having used it ever.

The Black Goddess is a carbon fiber violin inlaid with gold leaf that was given to him by Wedenstein Austria in 2014. It appears in the Beautiful Times video with Owl City

Cleopatra is a Yamaha SV-250 Silent Violin that Lindsey decorated with brilliants. He uses it for his concerts during Master of Tides, Shatter Me and Stars Align.

Excalibur is an Ernst Heinrich Roth Acoustic Violin from early 1900 and the first that Lindsey bought as a professional violinist. Her parents helped her buy it. It appears in the videos "Spontaneus Me", "Shatter Me", "Crystallize", "Senbonzakura" and many more.

Ingrid is a prop violin that Lindsey uses in videos where she needs to fill up with dirt or water or anything that other violins can not expose, she started using it as a prop because it was damaged and could not use it anymore. It appears in videos like Elements and Moon Trance.

Nero is a carbon fiber violin by Luis & Clark. Lindsey bought it for his concerts because she wanted a hard one because she sweat a lot and Excalibur was starting to get damaged, Nero is water resistant, it appears in the Minimal Beat video and Stars Align.

Paris is a blue Stagg with the form of an S of "Stirling" modified in the fretboard to make it an "L", was given by fans of France, usually used in Meet​&Greet's.

This is the first electric violin that Lindsey had, it's a Yamaha SV-250 and she decided to immortalize it for a video by giving it a steampunk look with parts of trumpets that were placed on it. It appears in the Roundtable Rival video. Before being transformed he appeared in Master of Tides, Shadows, Spontaneous Me and Violin Rock.

Wall-E is a Yamaha violin decorated with neon trape, now white. Lindsey has used it in Shatter Me Tour shows, during Swag, Night Vision, Heist and We Are Giants; and in The Music Box Tour during Ascendance, Senbonzakura, Night Vision, Moon Trance and Master Of Tides.

Bushwacker is a Yamaha YVN-500s. Lindsey uses it during some songs in the Music Box Tour shows like Shatter Me, Beyond The Veil and Phantom of the Opera, etc.
